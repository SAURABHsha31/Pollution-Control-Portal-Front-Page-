POLLUTION CONTROL PORTAL

COME TOGETHER, MAKE GREEN INDIA

Purpose

This WEB application is useful for society as it can help the society to keep the NGOs, volunteers and social workers together to take the responsibility to make the polluted area clean and give all the assistance to them by donation and proper invigilance. It can help us to make this process more reliable by generating the proper acknowledgement containing the fund from donation given and the time period in which the process has been done.

Proposal

This system will work like municipal corporation of a city. It will generate proposal, receipt and acknowledment of completion of work and budget of that process .

Scope

It is a multi user web application with both client side and server side.
With the help of this app NGOs and social workers can be connected and
share the information about the polluted area near their locality.
It will provide a platform for common man to inform the authorities about
the polluted areas nearby them to be the part of SWACCH BHARAT MISSION.
It will take the sponsors together and give a direction to them to donate
for clean India.
It will help the country to be cleaned as soon as possible as it can become
the alternative of Municipality.
